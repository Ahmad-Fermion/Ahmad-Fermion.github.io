About

I’m Ahmadreza Rajabi, a PhD candidate in Computational Chemistry at the University of California, Irvine (UCI). My research focuses on electronic structure theory and materials science, with a special interest in f-block organometallic complexes and heavy-element chemistry. With 17 peer-reviewed publications in journals such as JACS and Chemical Science, I collaborate with leading groups to advance chemical reactivity and material properties.
